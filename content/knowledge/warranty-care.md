# Warranty and Care

Warranty coverage:
- Standard coverage: 24 months
- Extended coverage (frames and drive systems): up to 60 months depending on category

Care guidance:
- Wipe surfaces with a soft, damp cloth
- Avoid abrasive cleaners on finished steel
- Store adjustable components in their base trays
- Use protective mats under heavy equipment

Assembly:
- Most products include guided assembly instructions
- Two-person lift is recommended for racks and treadmills
- White glove setup is available at checkout
